The Go-Ranch Profit Model

We manage the entire lifecycle of the Sandalwood plantation, from planting high-quality saplings to the final, regulated harvest 15 years later. This is a **hands-off investment** for you. Your initial purchase covers all costs—including **15 years of zero-cost maintenance, security, and expert care.**

Upon harvest, the profits are split transparently: **50% goes directly to the plot owner(s)**, and 50% covers the cost of harvesting, legal permits, processing, and company profit. Given Sandalwood's consistently appreciating market value, the returns are substantial.

Security & Scientific Management Guarantee

GPS-Chipped Tree Security

Every single Sandalwood tree in the Go-Ranch plantation is embedded with a **GPS-enabled chip**. If a tree is tampered with or moved, an instant alert is sent to our on-site security team, ensuring quick and decisive action against theft. Your asset is guarded 24/7.

IWST Expert Supervision

The plantation is **scientifically managed** by experts trained by the **Institute of Wood Science and Technology (IWST)**, a Central Government institute. This ensures best practices in irrigation, fertilization, and minimal tree mortality for maximum yield.

15 Years Free Maintenance

You pay nothing extra for 15 years. We cover all costs associated with plant care, security, water, labor, and compliance. Your investment is protected from future operational cost inflation.
