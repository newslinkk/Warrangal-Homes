Sandalwood Plantation Scheme (The 50-50 Agreement)

This section details the 15-year Sandalwood Agreement, including maintenance, profit sharing, and critical risks.

3.1. Plantation Service:

The Promoter agrees to plant and maintain 10 Sandalwood (Santalum album) plants per 120 Sq Yds (1 Unit) of the Plot for a period of fifteen (15) years from the date of planting ("Maintenance Period").

3.2. Maintenance Fee:

The maintenance, care, security, and scientific management of the Sandalwood plants during the Maintenance Period shall be provided free of cost to the Buyer.

3.3. Profit Sharing:

At the end of the Maintenance Period, or at the time of legal harvest (whichever is later), the Promoter will coordinate the harvesting, processing, and sale of the heartwood. The net profit realized from the sale of the Buyer’s trees will be shared on a 50% (Buyer) to 50% (Promoter) basis.

3.4. Security & Transparency:

The Promoter assures the use of GPS-enabled chips or similar technology for tree tracking. The Buyer will be provided with reasonable documentation regarding the planting date, tree counts, and harvest proceedings.

⚠ 3.5. Critical Risks and Disclaimers

  • No Guarantee: The Promoter does not guarantee the quantity or quality of the yield, the market price of sandalwood at the time of harvest, or a specific financial return (ROI).
  • Force Majeure: The Promoter is not responsible for losses to the plantation caused by natural calamities (e.g., floods, fire, severe drought), governmental actions, unforeseen pest infestations, or any events beyond the Promoter’s reasonable control. In such events, the Promoter will use reasonable efforts to mitigate losses but the 50-50 profit sharing will apply to any residual yield.
