Go Ranch Resort Sandalwood Plots

Policy Sections

1. Information We Collect

We primarily collect information that helps us manage your inquiries and market the Go Ranch Resort Project.

A. Information You Provide Directly

This includes personal information you voluntarily submit to us, primarily through inquiry forms, booking forms, or direct communication (email/phone). This information may include:

  • Contact Data: Your name, email address, phone number, and mailing address.
  • Inquiry Details: Questions, comments, or specific interests regarding plot sizes, investment goals, or the Sandalwood Scheme.
  • Transaction Data: Details related to potential or confirmed plot bookings or purchases (excluding sensitive financial data, which is handled through secure payment gateways).

B. Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain technical data using cookies and similar technologies. This data helps us understand site performance and user behavior:

  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the site, such as the pages you view, the time spent on pages, and the links you click.
  • Device Data: Information about the device and browser you use, including IP address, operating system, and browser type.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following core purposes:

  • To Respond to Inquiries: To contact you with requested information about the Go Ranch Resort Project, plot availability, pricing, and the Sandalwood Scheme.
  • For Marketing and Communication: To send you newsletters, promotional materials, updates on the project, and information about special offers, based on your stated interests.
  • To Process Transactions: To facilitate and manage the plot booking and purchase process, including necessary legal documentation and communication regarding payments and registration.
  • To Improve Our Website: To analyze usage patterns and technical data to enhance the user experience, optimize site performance, and ensure our content is effective.

3. Disclosure of Your Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties for their independent marketing or promotional purposes. We may share your information with the following parties only as necessary:

  • Affiliated Companies: With companies and entities associated with USM Infra that are directly involved in the development, legal, or sales aspects of the Go Ranch Resort Project.
  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party vendors and agents who perform services on our behalf, such as customer relationship management (CRM) platforms, email service providers, and digital marketing agencies. These parties are contractually obligated to keep your information confidential and use it only for the purposes for which we disclose it to them.
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law, court order, or governmental regulation, or when we believe disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, property, or safety, or the rights, property, or safety of others.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures designed to protect your personal information from accidental loss, unauthorized access, misuse, alteration, and disclosure.

  • All data collected through our website is stored on secure servers behind firewalls.

⚠ Security Disclaimer

However, no security system is impenetrable. We cannot guarantee the absolute security of our databases, nor can we guarantee that the information you supply will not be intercepted while being transmitted over the Internet. Any transmission of personal data is at your own risk.

5. Your Choices and Rights

You have control over your personal information:

  • Opt-Out of Marketing: You can choose to stop receiving marketing or promotional emails from us by following the unsubscribe link provided in those emails. Even if you opt out of promotional emails, we may still send you non-promotional communications, such as those related to your existing purchase or inquiry.
  • Cookies: Most web browsers are set to accept cookies by default. You can usually choose to set your browser to remove or reject browser cookies. Please note that removing or rejecting cookies could affect the availability and functionality of our website.
  • Access and Correction: You may contact us to request access to or correction of any personal information that you have provided to us.

6. Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or relevant laws. When we make changes, we will post the revised policy on this page with a new "Effective Date." We encourage you to review this policy whenever you access our site to stay informed about our data handling practices.
