Your Go-Ranch Digital Hub

The **USM My City Customer App** is your gateway to managing your investment and lifestyle.

Key Features & Amenities Managed

Project Progress Tracker

View real-time development updates, high-resolution photos, and videos of the DTCP plot development and resort construction progress.

Sandalwood Dashboard

Monitor the growth, health status, and projected harvest value of your **GPS-tagged sandalwood trees** directly on your dashboard.

Payments & Documents

Securely check your payment history, download receipts, view outstanding dues, and access your registered **Sale Deed** and agreements.

Resort Booking & Access

Book overnight resort cottages, check availability for the Conference Hall, or reserve slots for amenities like the Beach Pool and Mini Golf.

Direct Customer Support

Instantly raise service requests, submit maintenance queries, or chat directly with the Go-Ranch/USM support team for quick resolution.

Events & Announcements

Stay informed about upcoming events, weekend activities at the resort, and important announcements regarding the project timeline.
